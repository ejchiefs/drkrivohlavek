Biography:

Amber's favorite color is olive green, which she says is much like her - very unique, yet at the same time neutral. She grew up with her four siblings near Creighton, where she attended school. Amber and her husband, Josh, farm near Coleridge, and she lists her "pets" as many cows, Farley, the dog, and a cat named Butters. She enjoys being with family and friends, cooking, camping, playing sports and watching movies. 

After attaining an associate degree in early childhood development, Amber joined the dental field in 2012 and absolutely loves it! She is the newest member of our team and primarily assists the doctors but helps out wherever needed.
