Biography:

Vicki grew up in nearby Pilger and graduated from Wisner-Pilger High School. She began her dental assisting career in our office in 1990. She holds certificates in coronal polishing and dental x-ray.

Her son, Jason, and daughter-in-law, Crystal, have two children, while her daughter, Missy, and son-in-law, Dave, have four children. Vicki likes bowling, golfing, fishing and boating but especially enjoys spending time with her grandchildren.
