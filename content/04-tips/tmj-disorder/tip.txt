Title: Temporomandibular Joint (TMJ) Disorder Instructions

----

Text:

Rest the joint

* Eat a soft diet for two weeks: for example soups, mashed potatoes, jello, and pudding.
* AVOID steaks, raw vegetables, raw fruits, caramels, etc.
* Do NOT chew gum.

Use a heating pad twice a day

* 15 minutes in the morning, 7 1/2 minutes on each side
* 15 minutes in the evening, 7 1/2 minutes on each side

Take Ibuprofen (Motrin, Advil)

* 400mg to 800mg 3 or 4 times a day for 10 days
* Do not exceed 2400mg in 24 hours
